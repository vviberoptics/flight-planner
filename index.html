iPhone + RC-N2 Workflow

1. Draw area or upload KML. Adjust settings. Grid auto-generates.

2. Click DJI Fly KMZ to download mission file.

3. On iPhone: DJI Fly → Waypoints → save 2 dummy points → exit.

4. Files app → On My iPhone → DJI Fly → wayline_mission → replace newest .kmz with yours (rename to match).

5. Reopen DJI Fly → Waypoints → open mission → fly.

6. Camera: 12MP, Hyperlapse Free 2s interval, gimbal -90°, manual exposure, WB locked.

Display may show curved lines — drone flies straight. This is normal.
